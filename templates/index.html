How It Works

Our model is trained using a combination of supervised learning and reinforcement learning from human feedback.

1

Supervised Fine-Tuning

We start with a base model and train it on a high-quality dataset of conversations where human AI trainers demonstrate the desired behavior.

2

Reward Modeling

We collect comparison data by sampling several model responses to a prompt and having a human labeler rank them from best to worst. This data is used to train a reward model.

3

Reinforcement Learning

The reward model is used to further fine-tune the AI using a reinforcement learning algorithm. This process optimizes the model's policy to generate better, more helpful responses.

Limitations & Transparency

We believe in being open about our technology. While powerful, Phantom AI has limitations to be aware of.

  • May occasionally generate incorrect or nonsensical information. Always verify critical facts.
  • The quality of the response can be sensitive to the phrasing of your prompt. Rephrasing can often yield better results.
  • Knowledge is not updated in real-time and is limited to the model's last training date.
